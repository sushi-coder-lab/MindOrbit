# 🌍 The Problem

Every year, millions of students struggle with one simple question:

> **"What should I do next?"**

They are overwhelmed by endless career options, conflicting advice, outdated information, and a lack of personalized guidance.

Most career guidance platforms stop after recommending a career.

But a recommendation alone doesn't build a future.

Students need someone who understands them, teaches them, motivates them, tracks their progress, and walks beside them until they achieve their goals.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

## Meet **MindOrbit**

MindOrbit is an **AI-powered Career & Learning Companion** that doesn't just tell students *what* to become—it helps them discover *who they are* and *how to become it*.

Instead of generic advice, MindOrbit creates a completely personalized journey for every student based on:

* 🎯 Career Goals
* 🧠 Interests
* 💪 Strengths
* 📚 Learning Style
* 🧩 Personality
* 📈 Skills
* ❤️ Passion
* 🚀 Aspirations

It acts as a personal AI mentor available **24×7**.

---

# ✨ Our Vision

> **To ensure that no student has to navigate their career journey alone.**

MindOrbit is designed to become a lifelong AI mentor that supports students from self-discovery to career success.
